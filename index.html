About Me

I am an aspiring software engineer with a passion for all things tech related. I find the world of programming to be extremely intriguing, and I am constantly seeking to expand my knowledge and skills in this field. Considering the rate at which technology advances and new programming languages are developed, learning within this field is perpetual. I am particularly interested in web development, and I enjoy creating dynamic and interactive websites. In my free time, I like to work on personal projects, explore new technologies, and contribute to open-source initiatives.

Personal Hobbies

My personal interests are the polar opposite of my professional interests. I enjoy spending time outdoors, hiking, and exploring nature. I've found that letting all the noise throughout the day fade away while encapsulated in nature presents a mental clarity that is hard to replicate. I also enjoy reading, particularly books on psychoanalysis and philosophy. I find that these subjects provide a unique perspective on the human experience and help me understand myself and others better.
